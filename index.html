Who am I?

My name is Tyler Rubini and I am a current student in Fleming College's Geographic Information Systems post-graduate program. I expect to graduate in June of 2024 and then seek employment in the Canadian GIS industry. I possess an Honours B.A. in Geography and Environmental Studies and I am very excited to add a valuable post-graduate certificate on top of that. I have extensive retail experience, having spent nine years working primarily as an evening receiver at Rona (formerly Lowes) in Hamilton, where my supervisors regarded me as a very valuable employee. However, I decided that it was the right time to continue my education, which lead to me resigning in August 2023 to attend Fleming. I'm looking forward to moving into a new career that will allow me to apply knowledge of mapping and geography on a regular basis.

Outside of my professional life, I have an array of interests and hobbies. These include cycling, reading, going to the theatre, attending comedy shows, playing/running tabletop role-playing games (such as Dungeons and Dragons), and collecting/applying nail polish. I was born, raised, and normally live on the Niagara Escarpment in Hamilton (known locally as "the Mountain"), a reasonably walkable suburb with very good public transit access and a vast array of easily-accessible hiking and biking trails. This makes it very easy to get out and enjoy the outdoors, which I try to do as much as possible. Beyond that, I enjoy spending time with my family, which consists of my dad, two brothers, and a sister. I also have my mom and stepdad, who recently moved to rural New Brunswick after over a decade in Brantford, and there are several family cats at home as well.
